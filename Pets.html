Pet Abuse


There are a lot of pet owners in the world. Some people love their pets, but others will abuse their pets.
Abuse owners will be horrible things to their pets that may lead to death. There are two kind
of animal cruelty, active and passive cruelty. Active cruelty are animals that intent to harm to an animal creating
immediate pain and suffering. Passive cruelty are creating prolonged suffering. Some teens will use pet just for a prank or for fun
that can go wrong and horrible injury. Animal abuser are five times more likely to commit violent crimes against people.
Based on the F.B.I analyzed on the lives of serial killer, they stated that most killers have killed
or tortured animals as children. Pets will feel a lot of pain and fear by owners. It will cause them to have
disorder like being aggressive to people, experience sadism, against human, and etc.
Pets are like human too. They breath, drink and eat like us.
They have a heart and have emotions like us humans.

Do not get a pet if your not going to love them.
